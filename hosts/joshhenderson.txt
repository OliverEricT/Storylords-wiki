===== Josh Henderson =====

{{ https://storylords.carrd.co/assets/images/image03.jpg?v=a304c192}}

**Josh Henderson** is one of the hosts of Storylords and creator of [[daranos:daranos|Daranos]]. Josh provides editing for the show and is known for being a [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Red_hair|redhead]] and for being easily fire-able. Josh is a co-host of the YouTube channel [[https://www.youtube.com/@ContinueShow|Continue?]] and was a co-host of the former podcast [[deepknowledge:fantasyfiction|Fantasy Fiction]].
