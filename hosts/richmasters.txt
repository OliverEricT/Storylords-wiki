===== Rich Masters =====

{{ https://storylords.carrd.co/assets/images/image02.jpg?v=a304c192}}

**Rich Masters** is one of the hosts of Storylords and creator of [[omniverse:Omniverse|The Omniverse]]. He provides the intro for each episode as well as the episode art and is known for being [[https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/British_people|British]]. He also co-hosts the podcast Toon Hounds and writes and acts on the podcast T'Pon's Theater On Subspace.
