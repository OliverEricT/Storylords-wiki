===== Fantasy Fiction =====

**Fantasy Fiction** was a podcast that ran from September 6, 2013 to September 28, 2015. It was hosted by [[hosts:JoshHenderson|Josh Henderson]] and Dominic Moschitti. 71 main episodes were recorded, along with 4 "Wizard Hang" episodes, 2 specials and 1 "Chamber of Knowledge" episode. It is the origin of the realm of [[daranos:Daranos]].
